Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. Learn how we protect your data and respect your rights

Last updated: January 20, 2025

TL;DR: We don't collect, store, or transmit your photos. Everything happens in your browser. We use analytics to improve the service, and you can opt out anytime.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Photo Sheet Maker ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our website.

2. Information We Don't Collect

Your Photos: All image processing happens entirely in your web browser. Your photos are NEVER uploaded to our servers or any third-party service. When you upload a photo, it stays on your device.

Personal Information: We do not require you to create an account, provide your name, email address, or any other personal information to use our service.

3. Information We Collect

We use analytics services to understand how people use our tool and improve it. This includes:

Google Analytics

  • Page views and navigation patterns
  • Device type and browser information
  • Approximate geographic location (country/city level)
  • Actions taken (upload, download, option changes)
  • Your IP address is anonymized

Microsoft Clarity

  • Session recordings (showing cursor movement and clicks, but NOT your photos)
  • Heatmaps of where users click
  • General usage statistics

4. How We Use This Information

We use analytics data to:

  • Understand which features are most useful
  • Identify and fix bugs or errors
  • Improve the user experience
  • Make informed decisions about new features

5. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies for:

  • Analytics: Google Analytics and Microsoft Clarity use cookies to track your session
  • Preferences: Remembering your settings (optional, stored locally in your browser)

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may limit some analytics functionality.

6. Third-Party Services

We use these third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: Privacy Policy
  • Microsoft Clarity: Privacy Policy

7. Data Retention

Since we don't store your photos or personal data, there's nothing for us to retain. Analytics data is retained according to Google and Microsoft's policies (typically 26-50 months, anonymized).

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Use our service without providing any personal information
  • Opt out of analytics tracking using browser extensions like Google Analytics Opt-out
  • Clear cookies from your browser at any time
  • Use ad blockers or privacy tools to prevent tracking

9. Children's Privacy

Our service does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has used our service, please note that no personal data is collected or stored.

10. Security

Since all processing happens in your browser:

  • Your photos never leave your device
  • We use HTTPS to secure the connection to our website
  • No photo data is transmitted over the internet

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: aniltv06@gmail.com

13. GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

If you are in the European Union:

  • We do not process or store your photos (Article 5 - Data Minimization)
  • Analytics data is our legitimate interest (Article 6(1)(f))
  • You can opt out of tracking at any time
  • Your data is not sold or shared except with analytics providers

14. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected (see Section 3)
  • Request deletion of personal information (we don't store any)
  • Opt out of the sale of personal information (we don't sell data)
